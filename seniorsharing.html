Tips for freshies :3

  • Have a good playlist! Helps to survive coding and studying.

  • Sleep enough and have a good study/work and life balance.

  • Don't do anything last minute. Coding will always have hickups so dont save it for last.

  • Always have a backup copy of your work either in an external hard drive or on the cloud such as Github.

  • Learn industry tools and certifications.
